Process

How Voltiq Labs works with you

1

Share your requirement

Send your project title, branch, semester, and any instructions from your guide or college. You can also send a screenshot of the assignment.

2

Get project options & quote

We suggest 2–3 project options (or refine your idea), estimate cost, and tell you the delivery time.

3

Development & updates

We build the circuit & code, share small updates (photos or short videos), and make minor changes if needed.

4

Handover & explanation

You receive the project kit, key documentation points, and an explaining session so you can confidently face your lab, demo, and viva.

“At Voltiq Labs, our goal is simple: you should understand your project well enough to explain it in your own words, not just show a working kit.”

  • We don’t sell random kits without explanation.
  • We try to keep component choice available in local markets.
  • We avoid copying projects blindly; we adjust design to your level.
  • Basic documentation help is included in most projects.

FAQ

Common questions students ask

Will I get explanation of the project?
Yes. Voltiq Labs explains the block diagram, working principle, and basic code flow so you can confidently answer viva questions.
Can you create exactly the same project given by my guide?
In most cases, yes. Share the title and any document from your college. If it’s unrealistic in your time/budget, we’ll suggest a practical alternative.
Do you provide complete report?
We provide key points, block diagram, and references. You should prepare the final report in your own words based on your college format.
How much advance should I pay?
Usually 30–50% advance is needed to start the work (for components). Remaining can be paid during handover.
